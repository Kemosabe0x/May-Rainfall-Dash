June Prognostic Insights

Heat Amplification

Missing May rain removes evaporative cooling. Solar energy will directly heat dry soils, raising expected June averages by +2.5°F to +4°F.

High-Pressure Ridging

The atmospheric block that kept May dry is persisting. Expect extended stagnation, trapping heat and degrading urban air quality early in the month.

Flash Flood Risk

Baked, hardened topsoil acts like concrete. When isolated thunderstorms do occur, surface runoff velocity will be 30% higher than normal.
